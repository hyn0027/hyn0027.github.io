Seeking Participants for an Interview Study on ML-Powered System Safeguards

We are conducting an interview study to explore how practitioners approach safeguards for risks in ML-powered systems. This is part of a research study. Participants will discuss safeguards in (1) an ML-powered system we have built and (2) an ML-powered system they have worked on.

Who can participate?

  • 18 years or older
  • Have experience working on ML-powered systems and willing to discuss your experience during the interview
    (No confidential information will be requested during the interview)
  • Capable of coding in Python
  • Communicate in English

Compensation

Each participant will receive a $30 USD Amazon gift card after completing the study.

Procedures

If you decide to participate, you will complete a sign-up survey below (approximately 5 minutes to finish) to sign the consent form and collect information we will be discussing in the interview. For the interview, we will set up a time (a 60-90 minute slot) at your convenience. The study would be audio and screen recorded to compile accurate information about how you perform the task. We will not record any video of you. Anything you tell us will be confidential and we will not release any information without de-identifying the data.
